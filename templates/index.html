The Magical Kingdom Quest

Pick 2–4 Heroes

First to reach Space 54 (The Golden Castle) wins the Magic Crown. Beware dragons, traps, and extremely rude terrain.

Select at least 2 heroes to begin.

How It Works

  • Roll 1d6 → Move that many spaces → Resolve the tile.
  • Star: Answer a Magic Question. Correct = +2 spaces.
  • Dragon: Get shooed! -3 spaces (unless you shield/bribe).
  • Crystal: Draw a Magic Card.
  • Trap: Stunned! Skip next turn (unless you shield/disarm).
  • Exact win: Reach Space 54. If you overshoot, you BONK the castle wall and bounce back.
