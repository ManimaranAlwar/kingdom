The Magical Kingdom Quest
A comical race to the Golden Castle (bring coins, courage, and snacks).

Pick 2–4 Heroes

First to reach Space 30 (The Golden Castle) wins the Magic Crown. Beware dragons, traps, and extremely rude terrain.

Select at least 2 heroes to begin.

How It Works

  • Roll 1d6 → Move that many spaces → Resolve the tile.
  • Star: Answer a Magic Question. Correct = +2 spaces.
  • Dragon: Get shooed! -3 spaces (unless you shield/bribe).
  • Crystal: Draw a Magic Card (loot, chaos, or suspiciously helpful raccoons).
  • Trap: Stunned! Skip next turn (unless you ward/disarm).
  • Exact win: Reach Space 30. If you overshoot, you BONK the castle wall and bounce back.
